My Four Favorite Games - A Description

Dark Souls 3

The Dark Souls of the Dark Souls Franchise.

 
Splash Image Link  |  Gameplay Image Link

The Dark Souls franchise is known to be difficult. Their latest installment, Dark Souls 3, is no different. It is so hard that many people compare difficult games in different genres to it, saying things like "This is the Dark Souls 3 of Shooters". Many regard it as the best of the franchise, as it keeps the difficulty of previous entries without feeling clunky. It's hard to start though, as the game provides almost no instructions on how to play.

Vermintide 2

New York City Sewer Simulator.

 
Splash Image Link  |  Gameplay Image Link

Vermintide 2 is a hack and slash action RPG set in the Grim Dark world of Warhammer. The Ratmen are spewing forth from their under cities, invading the Empire of Man. It is up to your band of unlikely allies to squash the rat hordes, one axe blow at a time. This game plays like a dungeoncrawler, and gets quite difficult, especially on higher difficulties.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Thank god they can't recreate the smells.

 
Splash Image Link  |  Gameplay Image Link

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a cowboy action drama game detailing the life of Arthur Morgan, a cowboy on the run from the sins of his past and encroaching civilization. Set during the twilight of the cowboy era, this cowboy shoot-em-up blends realism, action, and natural speldor into one seamless package. In RDR2, when you're not robbing a bank or saving your gang members, you have the freedom to explore the wild west by fishing, hunting, gambling, and so much more.

Hearthstone

Kinda like a slot machine for kids!

 
Splash Image Link  |  Gameplay Image Link

Hearthstone is a collectible card game geared toward children. It involves extensive microtransactions, but is ultimately free to play. Card games have always been one of my favorite types of games, and Hearthstone has been my favorite of the genre for years now. Aside from the aggressive microtransactions (again, in a game geared for kids), its load of fun!
